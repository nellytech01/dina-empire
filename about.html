Who We Are

Founded by Oluwanifemi Akinrinlola

Dina Empire is one of the pioneers and leaders in quality custom-fit, made-to-order suits, shirts & african traditional wears. We remain unique in our approach, combining research in designs, technical innovation with premium materials and tasteful clothing design The customer is at the heart of our unique business model which begins from design, production, distribution, and sales through our store – experience centers, social media, website and other ecommerce platforms. Quality and excellence are at the heart of our job. Our JIT (Just in Time) process entails a fast and disciplined approach from idea conceptions, research, designs, cutting, sowing, packaging to distribution. Our wears are produced to reflect a fine and well detailed craftsmanship and elegance that suits the aesthetic taste required for a positive and lasting impression.
